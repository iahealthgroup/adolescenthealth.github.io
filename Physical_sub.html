Physical Health

Headache How? Headaches can be from many different things, like stress, sickness, not getting enough sleep, and many more factors. *Remember: The symptoms aren’t the same for everyone. Symptoms: Pounding sensation Is slightly hurting It is mild - hurts more but is still manageable Is severe - can’t stand the pain, hard to focus Hydrate - drink lots of fluids! Treatment: *If possible, go see a doctor. What are some things you can do at home besides pain medications? Warm or cold compress Sleep more Regular sleeping schedule Exercise Eat healthily - diet is very important! Massage the areas that hurt Get some rest Stay hydrated - water is key! Lower/close the lights Mental health in adolescents affects the way they act, think, and feel.
