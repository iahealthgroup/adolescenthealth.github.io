Headache

Headaches can be from many different things, like stress, sickness, not getting enough sleep, and many more factors. *Remember: The symptoms aren’t the same for everyone.

Symptoms:

Pounding sensation

Is slightly hurting

It is mild - hurts more but is still manageable

s severe - can’t stand the pain, hard to focus

Treatment: *If possible, go see a doctor.

What are some things you can do at home besides pain medications?

Warm or cold compress

Sleep more

Regular sleeping schedule

Exercise
