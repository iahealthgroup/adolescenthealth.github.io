Physical Health

Asthma How? Asthma can be passed down from family members, which would be through genetics or environmental factors. Sometimes it can even be both! Symptoms: Short of Breath (SOB) Hard to breathe - your lungs can’t open enough to get a lot of air in Coughing Wheezing - high-pitched noise because your airways are getting smaller (constricted) Feeling chest tightness Treatment: The treatments are typically prescribed because in asthma it is an internal problem. So, you will most likely have to go to a doctor. Nebulizer Inhaler Non-medication options once you use the medication: Exercise Diet
