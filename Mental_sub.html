Mental Health

Mood Swings: When emotions can range from happiness to anger to sadness in a matter of seconds, also known as emotional highs to emotional lows.

PTSD: A condition where an individual may be overwhelmed by intrusive/traumatic events. Examples include recurrent distress, anxiety, traumatic event, and flashbacks.
